## Scope Summary

Replace 4x ABB IRB 4400L/30 cavity wax process robots in North Cell 3 with new functionally equivalent robots. Full turnkey delivery including:

- 4x replacement robots (functionally equivalent to IRB 4400L/30)
- 4x robot controllers (MBUSI has 4 new IRC5 controllers available — 3HAC020536-015)
- 4x 7th axis rail assemblies (full replacement)
- All process equipment: fluid delivery, Festo valve packs, Leoni hose routing, EOAT, nozzle heads
- Integration with Beckhoff HMI, PLC architecture, ISRA vision, MBUSI SQL reporting
- Match Cells 1 & 2 standard architecture throughout

## Production Support Requirements (Section 18)

### Start-up Support
- Start-up period begins on first production day following shutdown
- **Minimum 2 full production weeks** of start-up support required before support can conclude
- Q-release must be achieved on all vehicle models before support concludes
- Contractor must maintain **sufficient qualified crew for each trade** during this period
- Contractor responsible for prompt correction of all deficiencies

### Spare Parts
- Contractor must maintain adequate inventory of spare parts for all new equipment
- From initial power-on through final acceptance
- Per Master Equipment Agreement (MEA)

### Training
- Comprehensive systems training for all MBUSI Maintenance teams
- Covers: equipment operation, troubleshooting, recovery procedures
- **Training outline must be submitted for approval 2 weeks prior** to training start
- Must include both classroom AND hands-on instruction using production equipment
- Materials in English
- Attendance records submitted to MBUSI upon completion

### Ramp-up Support
- Continued technical support during production ramp-up
- Process optimization and system fine-tuning
- **No additional cost** to MBUSI during this period

### Final Acceptance
- Granted only after:
  - All documentation approved by MBUSI Engineering
  - All training requirements fulfilled
  - All start-up support requirements met
  - All performance criteria achieved

## Key Gotchas

- **Silicone-free materials only** — 100% no silicone permitted
- All modifications due to robot selection are in supplier scope at no extra cost
- MBUSI-approved contractors only for subcontractors
- No deviations from ECM standards without prior written approval (EFG039)
- All materials must be on MBUSI pre-approved Paint Shop Compatibility List with MSC code
- Floor damage caused by contractor's work must be repaired at contractor's expense
- Change orders tightly controlled — kept to minimum
