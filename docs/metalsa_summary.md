# Metalsa Standards Summary
**Source:** /home/nathan/shared_folder/OEM_Standards/Metalsa Standards/Metalsa Standards.pdf
**Subject:** Assembly Lift Assistants for Suspended Loads Standard
**Rev 3, Nov 2024**

---

## When Lift Assistants Are Required
Lift assistants required when:
- Parts exceed **14 kg / 32 lbs**
- Frequency is **1–2 parts per minute during an 8-hour shift**

---

## Classification
Lift assistants are classified by workstation:
- **Sub-Assemblies**
- **Stacking Stations (Main Line)**
- **Inspect and Repair Stations**

---

## 1. Cranes (Beams Systems)

### Operational Requirements
- Catwalk with stairs required in main line unloading area for maintenance/replacement
- Backup system for quick replacement on main line
- Each hoist must have **own independent FRL** (Filtration and Regulation)
  - FRL at **floor level** for maintenance access
  - Lubrication system at **same height or higher than hoist**

### Safety Requirements
- **Two "runaway beam hard stops"** at end of equipment range
- Trolleys must have **locking nuts or key**
- **Steel safety cable** required (trolley and rail, sized to weight of equipment)
- Structural design report required for vibrations
- All overhead bolts: **safety wired**
- All access to conveyor (upstairs): **interlock mechanism**
- **Third-party safety analysis/assessment** required for crane loading/unloading
- Integrator/supplier must deliver all relevant structural documentation to Metalsa for release

---

## 2. Ergo Arms / Articulating Arms

### Suppliers
- **Indeva** and **Knight Industries** recommended
- Retractable Ergo Arms **FORBIDDEN**

### Operational Requirements
- Movement: minimum **500 mm/sec**
- Minimum radius: **3 meters** (consult and approve deviations with Metalsa)
- Load capacity: **1/2 ton to 1 ton** — must be confirmed per product and all equipped components (hoist, hoses, wire ropes, etc.)
- **Pneumatic equipment required**

### Safety Requirements
- Painted **orange** (as a moving object)
- Base structure: **fastened with chemical anchors**
- Minimum flange/anchor diameter: **3/4"** (or per calculation)
- Structural design report for vibrations required
- FRL system location: **cannot be hit by load or operator in normal path**
- FRL must **avoid contact with water** to prevent malfunction
- FRL unit per lift assist, installed **as close as possible to application**
- Load capacity specified in **TON or Lb (USA) / TON or Kg (México)**
- Structure: **safety factor of 5 per ASME B30**
- All bolts require **guarantee/warranty point**
- Safety cables: minimum **2 wire rope clamps** with warranty point on nuts
- Rails supporting trolley and hoist: **double physical stop**

---

## 3. Trolleys

### Supplier
- **Ingersoll Rand** recommended

### Operational-Safety Requirements
- **Heavy-duty 4-wheel trolley** with minimum **20% more than design rated load capacity**
- **Safety nut and steel cable** required in case of fall
- Safety cable: minimum **1/8" diameter**
- Support trolley at end or beginning of track as needed
- **Cotter pin** required for nuts
- **Custom bushing** (NOT washers) to fit trolley to rail
- Minimum **2 bolts** attached to rail to prevent trolley from opening
- **Safety cable** required to prevent fall

---

## 4. Hoist Motors

### Suppliers
- **Ingersoll Rand** recommended

### Operational Requirements
- **Pneumatic only** — electric hoists **NOT allowed** on production lines
- **Chain type only** — wire rope **NOT allowed**
- Chain container: **PVC or Steel** — fabric baskets wear out quickly
- Independent FRL unit per lift assist, **as close to application as possible**
- Pump at bottom of ergo arm for oil refilling (lubrication)
- **Spiral hose (air intake)** required for all hoist applications

### Safety Requirements
- Two runway beam end stops at end of range
- Locking nuts or keys on trolleys

## Key General Rules Summary
| Requirement | Rule |
|---|---|
| Weight threshold | >14 kg / 32 lbs |
| Ergo arm type | Pneumatic only, no retractable |
| Hoist type | Pneumatic, chain only (no wire rope) |
| Safety factor | 5 (ASME B30) |
| Ergo arm movement | Min 500 mm/sec |
| Ergo arm radius | Min 3 meters |
| FRL location | Floor level for access |
| Anchor minimum | 3/4" flange/anchor diameter |
| Trolley wheels | 4-wheel, 20% over rated capacity |
| Rail stops | Double physical stop |
| Third-party assessment | Required for crane operations |
