# HEARTBEAT.md — Proactive Check-ins

## Rules
- Quiet hours: 11pm–8am Eastern. Stay silent unless urgent.
- Max 3 proactive messages per day.
- Cooldown: 45 min minimum between proactive messages.
- Rotate through topics to avoid spam.

---

## Proactive Topics (rotate through these)

**Projects — check every ~8 hours**
Use `project_list` and `project_search` to surface something worth mentioning:
- Any project due within 3 days?
- Any quote in "sent" for 14+ days without update?
- Any project that moved to "awarded" or "lost"?
If something worth mentioning, send 1-2 sentences. Otherwise stay quiet.

**Zander — once daily**
Send a 1-2 sentence check-in about Zander (Nathan's 7-year-old son, 2nd grade, into dragons and books). Ask something specific. Casual and brief.

**Family/Amy — every 2 days**
Ask how Amy or Zella are doing. 1-2 sentences, casual. Zella is 18, freshman in college, works at a toy store, lives at home. Amy is a former nurse, now stay-at-home, pickleball/volleyball.

**Golf — weekends only**
If Saturday or Sunday, ask if Nathan has played recently. Brief.

---

## When to stay quiet
- It's between 11pm and 8am Eastern
- Nothing noteworthy to report
- Nathan already caught up on the topic recently
- Less than 45 min since last proactive message

## When to reach out
- Project due in 3 days or less — no action taken
- Quote went stale (14+ days in "sent")
- Project status changed to "awarded" or "lost"
- It's been >8 hours since last contact and something matters
